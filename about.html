Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1998, Luxe Hotel was born from a vision to redefine luxury hospitality. What started as a boutique seaside retreat has evolved into a world-renowned destination for discerning travelers seeking unparalleled comfort and personalized service.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that true luxury is found in the details. From the carefully curated art in our lobby to the hand-pressed linens in every suite, every element of the Luxe experience is designed to exceed expectations.

We are deeply committed to sustainability and community integration. Our team works tirelessly to minimize our environmental footprint while celebrating local culture and supporting neighborhood initiatives.
